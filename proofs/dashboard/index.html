Methodology. All formulas displayed are computed using the unified evolutionary portability model formally proven in Lean 4 with Mathlib. The model derives R², AUC, and Brier score degradation from Wright-Fisher population genetics, with every step verified by machine-checked proof. The four evolutionary forces — genetic drift, LD decay via recombination, mutation erosion, and migration boost — compose multiplicatively because they act on independent aspects of the PGS-phenotype covariance. The temporal dynamics follow from the heterozygosity recurrence H(t+1) = (1-1/(2Ne))(1-θ/(2Ne)) H(t) and the LD recurrence D(t+1) = (1-r) D(t). See DGP.lean for the complete end-to-end pipeline proof.
